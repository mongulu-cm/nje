Our Projects

Tchoung-te

Objective: To allow community members to find associations near them or according to a specific theme by providing an exhaustive and interactive map of all Cameroonian associations in France.

Website: https://tchoung-te.mongulu.cm/ or https://ai.mongulu.cm (for the chat version)

Source code: https://github.com/mongulu-cm/tchoung-te

Manzi-mfa

Objective: To provide students looking for internships in IT with advice and resources to accelerate their search through an online platform that allows them to book a free 1-hour session with a worker in the relevant sector.

Website: https://manzi-mfa.mongulu.cm

Source code: https://github.com/mongulu-cm/manzi-mfa

Chama

Objective: To provide Cameroonian associations in France with a website template to allow them to quickly establish an online presence, as well as maintenance and hosting provided by us.

Website: https://aci.assos.mongulu.cm/ (pilot project with ACI: Association of Cameroonians of Isère)

Source code: https://github.com/mongulu-cm/chama
