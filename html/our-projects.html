Our projects

Each project addresses a concrete need and remains publicly documented, with a clear emphasis on openness, reuse, and community value.

Tchoung-te

Objective: help community members find Cameroonian associations in France by location or topic through an exhaustive and interactive map.

Website: tchoung-te.mongulu.cm or ai.mongulu.cm for the chat-based version.

Source code: github.com/mongulu-cm/tchoung-te

Manzi-mfa

Objective: give students looking for IT internships access to advice, resources, and a free one-hour exchange with an experienced professional.

Website: manzi-mfa.mongulu.cm

Source code: github.com/mongulu-cm/manzi-mfa

Chama

Objective: provide Cameroonian associations in France with a site template, plus maintenance and hosting handled by the collective.

Website: aci.assos.mongulu.cm, a pilot project with ACI.

Source code: github.com/mongulu-cm/chama
